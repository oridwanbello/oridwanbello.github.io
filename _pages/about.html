Hello! I am currently a PhD candidate in the Department of Animal & Avian Sciences at the University of Maryland, College Park. I am passionate about improving human and animal health. My doctoral research, under the supervision of Dr. Philip Johnson and Dr. Li Ma, investigated the prophylactic and therapeutic potential of public T cells in humans and animals.
